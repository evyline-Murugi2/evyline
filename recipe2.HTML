Kenyan Swahili Pilau

Ingredients (serves 6)

  • 2 cups basmati rice
  • 500g beef (cubed)
  • 3 cups beef stock
  • 2 large onions (sliced)
  • 3 garlic cloves (minced)
  • 1 tbsp pilau masala
  • 1 cinnamon stick
  • 4 cardamom pods
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds
  • 3 tbsp vegetable oil
  • Salt to taste

Cooking Process

  1. Wash and soak rice for 20 minutes, then drain.
  2. Heat oil in pot. Fry onions until caramelized (10 mins). Add garlic and spices, cook until fragrant.
  3. Add beef, brown all sides. Pour in 1/2 cup stock, cover and simmer until beef is tender (30 mins).
  4. Add remaining stock and bring to boil. Stir in rice, reduce heat to lowest setting.
  5. Cover tightly with foil then lid. Cook for 20 minutes without opening.
  6. Remove from heat, let rest for 10 minutes. Fluff with fork before serving.

Serving Tradition

Authentic Kenyan pilau is served with kachumbari (tomato-onion salad) and a wedge of lime. Perfect for weddings, Eid, and Sunday family gatherings.
